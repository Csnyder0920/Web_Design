Brick Oven Pizza(Brooklyn Style)

Ingredients Directions
1 Teaspoon active dry yeast Sprinkle yeast over warm water in a large bowl. Let stand for 5 minutes to proof. Stir in salt and cold water, then stir in the flour about 1 cup at a time. When the dough is together enough to remove from the bowl, knead on a floured surface until smooth, about 10 minutes. Divide into two pieces, and form each one into a tight ball. Coat the dough balls with olive oil, and refrigerate in a sealed container for at least 16 hours. Be sure to use a big enough container to allow the dough to rise. Remove the dough from the refrigerator one hour prior to using.
1/4 cup warm water Preheat the oven, with a pizza stone on the lowest rack, to 550 degrees F. Lightly dust a pizza peel with flour.
1 cup cold water Using one ball of dough at a time, lightly dust the dough with flour, and stretch gradually until it is about 14 inches in diameter, or about as big around as the pizza stone. Place on the floured peel. Place thin slices of mozzarella over the crust, then grind a liberal amount of black pepper over it. Sprinkle with dried oregano. Randomly arrange crushed tomatoes, leaving some empty areas. Drizzle olive oil over the top.
1 teaspoon salt With a quick back and forth jerk, make sure the dough will release from the peel easily. Place the tip of the peel at the back of the preheated pizza stone, and remove peel so that the pizza is left on the stone.
3 cups bread flower Bake for 4 to 6 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the crust begins to brown. Remove from the oven by sliding the peel beneath the pizza. Sprinkle a few basil leaves randomly over the pizza. Cut into wedges and serve.
6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
6 leaves fresh basil,torn

Double Tomato Bruschetta

Ingredients Directions
6 roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped Preheat the oven on broiler setting.
1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil In a large bowl, combine the roma tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper. Allow the mixture to sit for 10 minutes.
3 cloves minced garlic Cut the baguette into 3/4-inch slices. On a baking sheet, arrange the baguette slices in a single layer. Broil for 1 to 2 minutes, until slightly brown.
1/4 cup olive oil Divide the tomato mixture evenly over the baguette slices. Top the slices with mozzarella cheese.
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar Broil for 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
1/4 cup fresh basil, stems removed
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 French baguette
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Pasta Pompodoro

Ingredients Directions
1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 minutes or until al dente; drain.
1/4 cup olive oil Pour olive oil in a large deep skillet over high-heat. Saute onions and garlic until lightly browned. Reduce heat to medium-high and add tomatoes, vinegar and chicken broth; simmer for about 8 minutes.
1/2 onion, chopped Stir in red pepper, black pepper, basil and cooked pasta, tossing thoroughly with sauce. Simmer for about 5 more minutes and serve topped with grated cheese.
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 (10.75 ounce) can low-sodium chicken broth
crushed red pepper to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Chicken Cheese Steak

Ingredients Directions
1(5 ounce) can chicken chunks, drained or 1 small chicken breast Cook chicken and hot sauce together in a non-stick skillet over medium heat, stirring to coat chicken evenly with hot sauce, until evenly heated, about 3 mins.
1 tablespoon of hot pepper sauce Scrape Chicken into the center of the pan to form a similar shape to the hot dog bun or bread of your choice. Cut Cheddar cheese or cheese of your choice in half and place on top of the chicken; cook until the cheese is melted, 1 to2 minutes. Place the bottom of the hot dog bun or bread over chicken and cheese mixture, slide a spatula underneath, and invert onto a plate. Place hot dog bun or bread overtop of the cheese.
